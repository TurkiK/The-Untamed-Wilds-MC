WHAT IS THE UNTAMED WILDS?

The Untamed Wilds is a vast and immersive world where players create their own factions and gather members to obtain lost gear and fight mythical creatures. The server features a rich and dynamic economy, where players can trade and sell items in the marketplace.

The Untamed Wilds is a seasonal project and a constantly evolving and exciting world, offering endless adventure and opportunity for those brave enough to explore it.
